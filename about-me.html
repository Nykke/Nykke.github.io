Hello, I'm Nyketha! Web Developer in Training at General Assembly, lover of memes and a great story. I am a proud University of Richmond Spider Alumna, Go Spiders! I'm originally from the small country, Guyana, located on the Atlantic Coast of South America and have lived in the Northern Virginia area since high school.

I've always been a writer, whether it was scribbling poems in a notebook or journaling or blogging, I was sure to be writing something. It was through my love of writing via my blog at the time, which focused on skincare that I found myself frustrated with not being able to tweak my blog the way I wanted that I learned about coding. To be fair, I did stumble upon coding once in high school, it was offered as an extracurricular activity and during my first (and last) meeting, I met a not so helpful male teacher who didn't make me feel welcomed as I had zero experience working with code. So here we were again, twelve years later, with more resources on the internet and access to workshops, I discovered a love for coding. As a creative and naturally curious person, web development fits me like glove and I can't wait to improve my skills!
